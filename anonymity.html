Privacy & Security Toolkit

A curated list of privacy-respecting tools and services.

Recommended
  • Brave
    https://brave.com/
    Built-in ad blocking and strong privacy defaults.
  • LibreWolf
    https://librewolf.net/
    Firefox hardened for privacy, no telemetry.
  • Tor Browser
    https://www.torproject.org/
    Maximum anonymity through Tor network routing.
❌ Avoid
  • Chrome
    Heavy tracking and data collection.
  • Edge
    Microsoft telemetry and integration tracking.
  • Opera
    Ownership and privacy transparency concerns.
Recommended
  • Proton Mail
    https://proton.me/mail
    End-to-end encrypted, privacy-focused Swiss provider.
  • Disroot
    https://disroot.org/
    Community-driven, privacy-first alternative services.
  • Tutanota
    https://tutanota.com/
    Encrypted email with minimal data collection.
❌ Avoid
  • Outlook
    Microsoft data ecosystem integration.
  • Yahoo
    History of breaches and tracking.
  • Gmail
    Extensive data mining and profiling.
  • Yandex
    Russian jurisdiction and data concerns.
Recommended
  • Proton VPN
    https://protonvpn.com/
    No-logs, audited, privacy-respecting jurisdiction.
  • Mullvad
    https://mullvad.net/
    Anonymous accounts, accepts cash payments.
Iffy
  • NordVPN
    Heavy marketing, past server breach.
  • ExpressVPN
    Ownership transparency concerns.
  • Surfshark
    Corporate mergers reduce independence.
❌ Avoid
  • Free VPNs (except Proton Free)
    Often log, sell data, or inject ads.
